Creamy Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Directions
